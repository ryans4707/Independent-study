Highschool Survival Guide

      Dont Let Highschool Eat You Up!

      Freshmen Year

      Coming into this school as a freshman can be very difficult for many reasons. Highschool is a very different place compared to Junior Highschool and elementary school. This makes adjusting to the new environment hard for many. However, one of the biggest pieces of advice that I can give is to be yourself. You will always find people that are just like you or have the same interest as you. You must also put yourself out there, however. You can not be shy when it comes to introducing yourself to people. You should thus join any clubs that interest you or even join a sports team. I know that for myself, if it was not for my teammates on the baseball team, I would not have the great friends that I have now. When it comes to your classes, you should start highschool on a very good note. This is just the start of four years of hard work. Everything that you do from now to the end will always be kept on record so make sure that you make the choices that will benefit you and your future the most. I am not saying that you have to be a scholar and be a straight A student, but instead do your work to the best abilities. Do all the hard work now when you are young so that your future can be easy.

        Junior Year

        Junior year can be considered one of the hardest years in Higschool. This is when you are already adjusted to the highschool environment, and the work begins to get much harder. You must stay focused especially for this year. Try your best to maintain good grades in every class because this is the year that your grades go to the colleges that you apply to. On that note, make sure that you make it a huge priority to start researching colleges that you would possibly want to apply to during senior year.
